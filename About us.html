Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver person-first, accessible, and trusted care for people of all age groups with special needs, empowering families with the right support at the right time.

Our Vision

A world where every individual with special needs can learn, live, and thrive with dignity—supported by informed families, trained caregivers, and an inclusive community.

Our Story

Why we started

Our journey began with a simple, heartfelt observation: families caring for loved ones with special needs often carry invisible loads—coordinating therapies, balancing school and work, and searching for trusted help. When our founder, Ananya Sharma, supported a close relative with developmental needs, she experienced first-hand how fragmented the care system can feel.

We built Saarthi to change that—to be a steady companion for families. Today, we connect trained caregivers and therapists with households across different life stages—from early intervention to adult independence—so that every person receives consistent, compassionate care.
